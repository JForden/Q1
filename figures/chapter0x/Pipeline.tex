\caption{%
  The course data pipeline. Each stage transforms the raw market data
  into a form the machine learning model can use.
  \textit{Tick Data} captures every trade event; \textit{Dollar Bars}
  resample that stream into uniform economic time; \textit{Fractional
  Differentiation} enforces stationarity while preserving trend memory;
  and the \textit{Feature Matrix} encodes our trading hypotheses as
  measurable inputs. This chapter picks up at the final stage.%
}
